About us

Alexandria Intermediate Industries (MARCOGYPT)
Egyptian Shareholder Company

Marcogypt has established 1993 as the first carbon processor company in Egypt and the Arab area. Our main activity is providing the end users by their needs of Coal ready by changing some characteristics such as drying to reach the moisture content requested by the customers also changing the physical size to be the suitable for gaining the maximum benefits from using the product.

Since 1993 till 2010 we were the sole suppliers of Coal in Egypt for the hugest manufacturers of Iron & Steel such as Ezz Steel (ex-Alexandria National Iron & Steel Company, ANSDK).

Since 2009 we started a big plan to improve the productivity of our plant by revamping the equipment to increase the capacity for that we selected a suitable area in the new industrial zone in Amreya Alexandria to be near the main roads to avoid entering the Alexandria down town and starting to move by using the new advanced technology and machines imported from Italy and German also latest generation of laboratory equipment imported from Leco USA and Retsch Germany.

Our main aims from such revamping are:

  • Increasing the capacity to be 30 mt/h instead of 8mt/h as before
  • Updating the technology of Coal processing to reach to the ideal usage of input raw material and the perfect output production to comply with the specification requested by the customers
  • Following up the environmental requirements to avoid the pollution
  • Insure the quality control for input raw material and output product

Since 2012 we started working at the new location in Amreya industrial zone and with full capacity of 30mt/h and within the quality control designed for this plant

How we are achieving our targets

The main target is to produce our product under strict quality procedures to achieve the highest level of quality by continous analysis and inspection for the Anthracite Coal:

  • Firstly the raw Coal inspected and analysis before shipping by independent international third party inspection office such as SGS, INCOLAB, ALEXSTEWART ..... etc.
  • During discharging of raw Coal in the destination port, we repeat the above mentioned procedure in addition to our laboratory which equipped by highest technology of analysis equipment.
  • During processing and before delivering the final product to customers, we make a complete analysis by our laboratory to assure that the final product is exactly the requested of customers

Furthermore, our production lines which designed by the biggest German & Italian manufacturers and erected by the Turkish experience to ensure that the final product has the same characteristics requested by the customers

OUR TARGET is the 100% satisfaction of our customer
